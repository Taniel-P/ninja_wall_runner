Torii gate, lanterns, mist, and other forest decoration are drawn procedurally in code (no external assets).

The city (Tokyo) theme group's entire visual style - night sky, parallax skyscraper skyline, neon haze, rooftop platform texture, neon signage, and the shrine-gate goal marker - is drawn procedurally in code as well (no external assets).

The mountain (Mt. Fuji) theme group's entire visual style - storm sky with lightning, jagged snow-capped peaks, storm clouds, cliffside clutter, icy rock platform texture, and the storm-gate goal marker - is also drawn procedurally in code (no external assets).

The temple (final) theme group's entire visual style - inferno sky with ember flares, crumbling pagoda skyline, a looming guardian silhouette that grows more visible across the group's 5 levels, ember haze, ruin clutter, charred platform texture, the escalating final gate, and spirit-flame decoration - is also drawn procedurally in code (no external assets).
